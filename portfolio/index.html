Experiences ¶

Google Summer of Code (May 2021 - August 2021) ¶

I was a student developer for TensorFlow organization and worked on a project to improve TF-GAN library. I worked on implementing a super-resolution GAN model named ‘ESRGAN’ and a text-to-image translation model named ControlGAN. The main aim was to update the library with more recent variants of GANs that have more applications. I also wrote Google Colaboratory tutorial notebooks for end-to-end training of these models that explored various functionalities of TF-GAN.

Eastern European Summer School (July 2021) ¶

I was selected for attending the EEML Summer School 2021 (virtual). This week long summer school covered various active research topics in the field of deep learning and reinforcement learning and were taken by some of the best ML researchers from various companies and universities including DeepMind, Facebook AI (FAIR), Google Research, University of Cambridge and McGill University. Apart from lectures there were also coding tutorials, journal clubs/reading groups and poster presentation that explored recent advancements in various areas of research such as Vision and NLP.
